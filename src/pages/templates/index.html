Who we are

MacGregor Construction is a family run business established by Malcolm MacGregor in 1973. With hard work through the years we built a company that has a great standing in many communities throughout the United Kingdom. We have seen many changes in the years we have been establish, the one thing that remains is our passion to build your projects to the high standards we have always been committed too.

Always dedicated & devoted.

Our main base of stay is the lovely town of Mold, North Wales, where we started all those years ago. We have grown since then to cover Cheshire, Manchester, Liverpool, London and many more areas. Where we have been providing highly skilled and vetted tradesmen & tradeswomen with the skills necessary to complete all projects. No matter how small or large your ideas maybe.

Domestic Construction

We provide everything that is needed for your ideas to be come a reality. We work with building control, architects, structural engineers, building surveyors. To design and build the best solution for you. That is safe, warm and inviting to you and your family. From a simple renovation to new builds we are there for you always dedicated & devoted.
